**Absolute Immunity Scope**
- Map states immunity extends to "conduct intimately associated with the judicial phase of the criminal process."
- Chapter quotes *Imbler*: "intimately associated with the judicial phase of the criminal process."

**Selective Prosecution Standard**
- Map: "discriminatory effect" + "discriminatory purpose" ("at least in part because of").
- Chapter: "*Wayte v. United States*, 470 U.S. 598 (1985), established the two-prong test … 'discriminatory effect' … 'discriminatory purpose' … the prosecutor acted 'at least in part "because of," not merely "in spite of,"' the discriminatory effect."
- ✅ Accurate

**Angelos Post-Script (First Step Act)**
- Map: "First Step Act (2018) eliminated § 924(c) stacking within a single indictment."
- Chapter: "The First Step Act of 2018 subsequently amended Section 924(c) to eliminate the 'stacking' of mandatory minimums within a single indictment—meaning that if Angelos were prosecuted today, he would face a maximum mandatory minimum of 10 years rather than 55."
- ✅ Accurate
