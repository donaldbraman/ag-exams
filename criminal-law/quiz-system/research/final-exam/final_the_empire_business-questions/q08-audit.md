**Q8.** To hold Jack liable for Kevin's actions at the restaurant, the government must first prove Kevin committed an attempted crime. Did Kevin's actions outside the restaurant constitute an attempt to commit property damage?

(a) Yes under the proximity test, because by parking, masking up, and grabbing a crowbar 50 feet away, he came dangerously close to completing the crime.
(b) No under the substantial step test, because dropping the crowbar and fleeing upon seeing Matthew constituted a valid, complete, and voluntary renunciation of criminal purpose.
(c) Yes under the substantial step test, because arriving at the scene armed with the necessary tools strongly corroborated his criminal purpose to break the windows. <!-- correct -->
(d) No under the proximity test, because Kevin had not yet swung the crowbar at the glass, meaning no actual harm was imminent to the building.
(e) Yes under the substantial step test, but only because he was carrying a loaded 9mm handgun in his pocket while approaching the commercial premises.

**Answer:** (c)

**Explanation:** The correct answer is (c). Under the MPC's substantial step test, arriving at the target scene with materials designed for the crime (the crowbar and ski mask) is a substantial step strongly corroborating the actor's criminal purpose. (a) is wrong because under the strict common law proximity test, courts often require the defendant to reach the very last act (like raising the crowbar); being 50 feet away in the parking lot might not satisfy proximity, making (c) the more undeniably correct application. (b) is wrong because Kevin fled only after seeing Matthew with a shotgun; renunciation must be voluntary, not motivated by a newly discovered threat of apprehension or resistance. (d) is wrong because the proximity test does not strictly require the very last physical motion (swinging the crowbar) in all instances, though it is closer than substantial step. (e) is wrong because the handgun is irrelevant to the substantial step for property damage; the crowbar and mask constitute the step.
