(d) is incorrect because RICO applies to wholly illegitimate operations, not just the infiltration of legitimate businesses. (b) is incorrect because moving drugs weekly for two years easily satisfies the continuity requirement. (a) is incorrect because an enterprise can consist of the associated individual defendants acting together.
