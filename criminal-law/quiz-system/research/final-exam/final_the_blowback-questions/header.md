# Scenario: Moretti Syndicate

### Facts

1. The District Attorney's Organized Crime Division established that Carmine, a Capo in the Moretti syndicate, convened a meeting with associates Benny, Dom, and Leo to propose the armed hijacking of a commercial electronics truck. Benny nodded silently during the pitch but took no part in the subsequent planning.
2. A week later, Carmine suspected associate Paulie was an FBI informant and lured him to his private boat to confront him, though without an initial intention to kill. During the confrontation, Paulie laughed, explicitly admitted he was an informant, sneered "You're washed up, old man," and quickly reached into his jacket.
3. Carmine, genuinely but unreasonably believing Paulie was drawing a weapon, made a split-second conscious decision to shoot, drew his gun, and fired. Paulie was unarmed; he was reaching for a cigar.
4. The following day, Carmine ordered Benny to discreetly dispose of fifty barrels of highly toxic chemical solvent, threatening, "If this isn't gone by tonight, you're going in one of the barrels." Benny believed the threat of death was imminent.
5. Benny read the warning label about lethal fumes but dumped the barrels in a residential ditch anyway, telling a friend: "There is a risk it creates a toxic cloud, but it is pouring rain. The rain should dilute it, and nobody jogs in this weather anyway."
6. The next morning, the rain stopped. Victor, a 65-year-old jogger, inhaled the concentrated chemical vapor cloud while running past the ditch and died of acute toxic asphyxiation.
7. Later that week, Dom asked Leo for a ride to the loading dock to hijack the electronics truck, offering $500. Leo knew Dom was carrying a loaded gun to commit the hijacking, but Leo refused to help plan the operation and just waited in the idling car.
8. As Dom approached the truck, he saw a police cruiser. Spooked, Dom decided he did not want to risk arrest, abandoned the plan entirely without touching the truck, and sprinted back to Leo's vehicle.
9. Dom jumped into the passenger seat and yelled, "Go! Cops!" Leo sped away. During the subsequent high-speed evasion, a pursuing police cruiser lost control at an intersection, fatally striking a pedestrian, Wendy.
10. Following the crash, police apprehended Dom and Leo. In the getaway vehicle, police found a vacuum-sealed kilogram of fentanyl hidden inside the center console.
11. Leo is the registered owner of the car, but Dom's duffel bag had been resting directly against the console during the pursuit, and Dom's fingerprints were on the console latch.

### Stem 1: Syndicate Operations and Environmental Crimes

The ADA wants a memo on the Moretti syndicate's internal operations and environmental crimes. Assess the homicide on the boat, the toxic dumping incident, and whether we can wrap this up as a RICO enterprise.
