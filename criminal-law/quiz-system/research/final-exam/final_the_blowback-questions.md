(c) is wrong because prior planning is not required under the Pennsylvania rule, and his general habit was unrelated to Paulie. (d) is wrong because the lack of an initial plan upon boarding is irrelevant if the intent crystallized in the ten seconds before the shot. (e) is wrong because while the use of a deadly weapon may infer intent to kill, it does not automatically legally presume premeditation.
