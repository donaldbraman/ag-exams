(c) is wrong because his use of the corporate payroll and structure is also sufficient; the fleet crossing regional lines is not the sole qualifying factor. (d) is wrong because the *Cedric Kushner* doctrine explicitly recognizes the distinctness between a sole owner and their incorporated business. (a) is wrong because mere knowledge without a true purpose to facilitate the crime is typically insufficient. (c) is wrong because strict liability does not apply to legal counseling or accomplice liability.
